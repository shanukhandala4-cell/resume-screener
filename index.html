How It Works

Three steps, powered by real NLP techniques

📄

1. Text Extraction

PDF.js parses your uploaded resume directly in the browser — no data is ever sent to a server. Your information stays completely private.

PDF.js · Browser API
🧠

2. TF-IDF Vectorization

Both texts are transformed into numerical vectors. TF-IDF weights each word by how frequently it appears in the document versus a corpus — giving important terms higher scores.

TF-IDF · Numerical Vectors
📊

3. Cosine Similarity

The angle between the two vectors is measured. Vectors pointing in the same direction (high similarity) yield scores near 100%, while divergent vectors score near 0%.

Cosine Similarity · ML Scoring
